About this app

This app has been built to demonstrate the architecture of an Angular browser app. The mission of the app is to provide departure information for public transport services in Budapest metropolitan area.

The full source code of the app is available on github: https://github.com/mkaboldy/bkk-departures. Feel free to clone, fork or report issues.

Privacy

We don't store any information about you.

Demos

For the best results, be sure you are in the vicinity of Budapest metropolitan area and allow your browser to know your location.

If that's not possible, the app will not be able to discover the nearby stations and services. In this case you can select the locations or departure boards you bookmarked earlier, or open any of the links below:
